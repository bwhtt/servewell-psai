## üìù Give Feedback

Your insights improve the methodology, catch errors, and ensure this work reflects reality.

### What Feedback Helps Most

**If you're a public servant:**
- Does your state's report accurately reflect your experience?
- Are we missing benefits, programs, or recognition initiatives?
- Do the vacancy rates and retention figures match what you see on the ground?
- What factors affect your job satisfaction that we're not measuring?

**If you're a researcher or policy expert:**
- Are there better data sources we should use?
- Do our statistical methods hold up to scrutiny?
- Are we interpreting findings correctly?
- What additional dimensions should we consider?

**If you're a state government employee:**
- Can you help us find non-public data (training budgets, detailed vacancy reports)?
- Can you clarify how specific programs work?
- Are there budget line items we're misinterpreting?

**If you're a citizen or advocate:**
- Does this data help you hold your state accountable?
- What information would make the reports more useful for advocacy?
- Are the recommendations realistic and actionable?

### How to Submit Feedback

**üìß General Feedback & Questions**  
Use our **[contact form](#)** for:
- Broad methodology suggestions
- Partnership inquiries
- Media requests
- Stories you think we should know
- General questions about the project

**üîß Technical & Data Issues**  
Use **[GitHub Issues](#)** for:
- Specific data corrections ("Maryland's teacher vacancy rate is actually X, not Y")
- Methodology suggestions with supporting evidence
- Formula or calculation errors
- Broken links or missing sources
- Requests for additional analysis

**üó∫Ô∏è State-Specific Feedback**  
Every state report page has a feedback section. Use it to:

## üí¨ Share Your Story: Humans of Public Service

The numbers tell part of the story. Your experience tells the rest.

**Humans of Public Service (HOPS)** is an initiative to celebrate and document the real people behind public service‚Äîthe teachers, social workers, firefighters, clerks, analysts, and countless others who make communities work.

### What We're Looking For

**Stories that show:**
- Why you chose public service
- A moment when your work made a real difference
- Challenges you've overcome
- What keeps you going despite obstacles
- How your state or agency supports you (or doesn't)
- What appreciation looks like in practice

### How to Contribute

**Share your own story:**  
**[Submit Your Story](#)** ‚Äì 300-500 words about your public service experience

**Nominate someone:**  
Know a public servant with an inspiring story? **[Nominate them](#)** and we'll reach out

**What happens next:**
1. We review submissions for clarity and authenticity
2. We may reach out with follow-up questions
3. Stories are published on ServeWell.ai (with your permission)
4. Stories may be shared with the broader HOPS initiative
5. Your contribution helps show the human side of public service data

**Privacy options:**
- Full name and photo
- First name and title only
- Anonymous (general role description only)

Your story could inspire the next generation of public servants or show policymakers why this work matters.

**üèõÔ∏è Policy Advocates & Nonprofits**

- Amplify findings to media and policymakers
- Provide feedback on how to make reports more useful for advocacy
- Partner on joint initiatives

**üíª Developers & Designers**
- Improve website functionality and user experience
- Create data visualizations and interactive features
- Build tools for state-by-state comparison
- Optimize mobile experience
- Contribute to open-source codebase

**üè¢ State Government Contacts**
- Provide access to non-public data (with appropriate permissions)
- Clarify budget line items and program details
- Validate our interpretations of policies

- Connect us with relevant departments and programs

**‚úçÔ∏è Writers & Storytellers**
- Help collect HOPS stories

### How to Start Collaborating

**Step 1: Introduce yourself**  
**[Fill out the collaboration form](#)** with:
- Your background and expertise
- Which area(s) you're interested in contributing to

**Step 2: We'll connect**

**Step 3: Choose your level of involvement**
- **Light touch**: Occasional feedback or one-off contributions
